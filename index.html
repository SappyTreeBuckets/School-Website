The School Library

The library was built with the school.

The library is where you can borrow books at the school. Students can even help out at the library as library helpers. They can sign up for this position at the the grades 7th and 8th. The school library consists of multiple books like graphic novel and non-fiction books. The library also has an abundance of fiction books.

The gym is usualy where you would have PE or your physical education are held. The school usualy has things called socials where you hangout and dance with your friends. Something similar to prom but it's not prom. The socials are held in the gym.
